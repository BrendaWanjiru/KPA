Welcome to Kenya Paediatric Association


The Kenya Paediatric Association (KPA) is a professional body dedicated to the health and well-being of children in Kenya.
KPA's mission is to promote the health and well-being of all children in Kenya through the advancement of
paediatric education, research, and clinical practice. KPA provides a platform for paediatricians to share knowledge,
collaborate on research, and advocate for policies that benefit childrenâ€™s health.

KPA organizes regular seminars, workshops, and conferences to keep paediatricians updated on the latest advancements in
child healthcare. The association also works closely with other health organizations and the government to ensure that
children in Kenya have access to the best possible healthcare services.
